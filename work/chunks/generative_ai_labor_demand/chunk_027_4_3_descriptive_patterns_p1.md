# 4.3 Descriptive Patterns

Webeginbydocumentingseveraldescriptivepatternsintheposting-levelexposuremeasure. These patterns serve two purposes. First, they show that generative AI exposure varies substantially across postings, seniority levels, occupations, industries, and time. Second, they motivate the decomposition analyses that follow by showing why aggregate changes in exposure may reflect both changes in the mix of posted jobs and changes in the task content of comparable jobs.
