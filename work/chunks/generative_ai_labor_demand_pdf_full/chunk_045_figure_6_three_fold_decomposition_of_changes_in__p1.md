Aggregate exposure rises relative to the 2021 baseline through early 2023, then turns negative beginning in Q3 of 2023 and declines through the end of the sample. The decomposition shows that this turning point reflects changes along both margins. Before Q3 of 2023, the composition effect is generally positive, indicating that the mix of postings shifts toward job cells that were more exposed at baseline. From that quarter onward, the composition effect becomes negative, indicating that posted hiring demand shifts away from cells that were more exposed in 2021. This
