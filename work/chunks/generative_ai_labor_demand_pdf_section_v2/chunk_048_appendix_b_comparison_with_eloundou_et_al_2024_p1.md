To benchmark our measure against the existing literature, we compare our posting-based exposure measure with the occupation-level exposure measure in Eloundou et al. (2024). Their measure is constructed from O*NET task data and assigns each occupation a single, time-invariant exposure score, whereas our measure is constructed from job postings and can vary across postings within the same occupation and over time. To make the two approaches comparable, we aggregate our posting-level exposure measure β p to the occupation level and compare it with the benchmark in Eloundou et al. (2024). Figure B1 shows that the full-sample occupation-level average of our measure is highly correlated with the benchmark in Eloundou et al. (2024). Thus, at the aggregate occupation level, our measure is broadly aligned with the benchmark in Eloundou et al. (2024).
