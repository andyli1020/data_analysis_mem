This figure provides a level-based benchmark for the decomposition reported in the main text. The figure confirms the same qualitative pattern as Figure 6. In the earlier part of the sample, the composition counterfactual tracks observed exposure closely, while the within-only counterfactual remains much closer to the 2021 baseline. This indicates that the early increase in aggregate generative AI exposure is driven mainly by compositional reallocation across job cells. After
